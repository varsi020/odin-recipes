Description

This recipe is a classic NY-style pizza like the ones you can find at any pizza place in Manhattan.

Ingredients

  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Bread flour
  • Instant yeast
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Combine flour, sugar, salt, and yeast
  2. Transfer dough ball to floured surface and knead until smooth ball is formed
  3. Divide dough into even parts
  4. Place in fridge and allow to rise
  5. Remove from fridge and shape into balls
  6. Allow to rest
  7. Bake
